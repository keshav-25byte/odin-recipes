Odin Recipes

This is a page where we find recipies claimed to be die for on the internet and try them so you don't need to

  1. Sweet Noodle Kugel

    This kugel recipe is the same style of kugel we'd get in a New York City area Jewish deli, and that my relatives would make for holidays. There seems to be endless variations of this noodle pudding now, but for me this is the authentic original version.

  2. Italian Grinder Pasta Salad

    This Italian grinder pasta salad has everything you want from your favorite grinder, in a classic rotini pasta salad—salami, ham, provolone, and a tangy olive oil dressing—in a batch big enough to serve a crowd.

  3. Cheesy Buldak Ramen Casserole

    This cheesy Buldak ramen casserole tames the heat—a little—with the addition of creamy mozzarella and American cheese. It's a quick and easy bake.
